Window Description

The top bar displays several details of your game.

  • Health: For every virus that hits you, you lose 20% of your health
  • Score: Your current score
  • Target Score: Score needed to reach the next level
  • Time: The time remaining in your current level
  • Level: Your current level
  • Ammo: Total number of bullets you have

Controls

The top bar displays several details of your game.

  • Use Up,Down,Left and Right keys to move your ship
  • Press the X or Z key to switch your camera
  • You may use a right click or space bar to shoot
  • Use Q and E to position your ship more forwards or backwards
  • You may use A,S,W, and D to maneuver

Home

Objective

To make it to the next level, destroy as many viruses as you can. Should you reach a levels target score, you win the level.
